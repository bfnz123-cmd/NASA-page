Crew-12 Mission Profile (Launched February 13, 2026)

This crew was launched from Cape Canaveral via a SpaceX Falcon 9 to replace an early medical evacuation. They are stationed for a 9-month duration.

Astronaut Profiles & Mission Facts
Astronaut Name Country / Agency Background / Role Key Mission Facts
Jessica Meir USA / NASA Marine Biologist Veteran; famous for previous all-female spacewalks.

Sophie Adenot France / ESA Helicopter Pilot Only the second French woman ever to fly in space.

Crew-13 Mission Manifest (Targeting September 2026)

NASA advanced this launch window to re-stabilize the standard 6-month crew rotation cycle. Read and Learn more about Crew-13

  • Jessica Watkins (Commander, NASA): Setting records as the first NASA astronaut to fly twice aboard a SpaceX Crew Dragon.
  • Luke Delaney (Pilot, NASA): Rookie pilot from Central Florida.
  • Joshua Kutryk (Mission Specialist, CSA): Rookie representing Canada.
  • Sergey Teteryatnikov (Mission Specialist, Roscosmos): Rookie cosmonaut.
